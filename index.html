✦ The artist

Hi, I'm Maria

I'm a painter based in my home studio, working mainly with acrylics and oils to create pieces that sit somewhere between the representational and the abstract. My paintings begin with a feeling — the quality of afternoon light, the colour of a field just before a storm — and evolve intuitively from there.

Every original I paint is completely unique and comes with a certificate of authenticity. My fine art prints are reproduced using museum-grade giclée printing on cotton rag, and are personally signed and numbered in limited editions.

Follow along for studio updates, new work, and a look behind the scenes.

How it works

From studio to your wall

🎨

Hand-painted or printed

Originals are painted by Maria in her studio. Prints are produced on a museum-grade giclée press on 300gsm cotton rag paper.

📦

Carefully packaged

Each work is wrapped in acid-free tissue, rolled or flat-packed in rigid board mailers, and shipped with tracking.

🖼️

Ready to hang

Originals come ready-to-hang. Prints arrive unframed — perfect for framing yourself or as a frameless wall piece.
